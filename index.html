About

Who we are and What is V.E.I.N-B

Very Exacting and Informative Nerd Bot (V.E.I.N – B) is a Medical Diagnosis Bot developed by Team Ven including Shuvam Pal and Kushagra Kartik Verma of Birla Bharati School. V.E.I.N serves the main motive of spreading the message of Cardiac health, inspired by the recent unfortunate event of Denmark’s Christian Eriksen’s sudden Cardiac Arrest while playing in the Euro Cup 2020.

  • How It Works
  • Start by typing in 'Hi'. The bot will help you after that.
  • Trace your probable heart defect.
  • If you feel that a checkup is required, then you can carry your generated report to your desired doctor.
  • V.E.I.N - B has been designed for spreading cardiac awareness and for assisting doctors. This is a system generated response and medications should not be taken with regard to this diagnosis. Taking help from a verified specialist is recommended.
  • You can also use V.E.I.N's mood tracker for further assessment.

Features

What we do offer

Mental Assessment

V.E.I.N can also determine the emotional status of users for a proper healthy lifestyle, both socially and mentally.

Cardiac Health Checkup

V.E.I.N works along a system of probability. It encourages users to determine the rationality of Cardiac defects in their body in a range of High, Mild or Low.

Recommends Desirable Doctors

Currently an alpha feature, the developers plan to integrate further processes for specialist recommendation.

Has a user-friendly and cheerful UI

V.E.I.N has a very user friendly integration and is extremely easy to use and understand.

Language Support

V.E.I.N currently functions in only Hindi and English languages but, can be easily extended to other languages easily

Provide Audio Aid As Well

V.E.I.N has been designed to cater to all parts of the society and is very useful for even the visually handicapped

Downloadable Report

Reports generated by V.E.I.N are downloadable and can be used for supporting doctors for quick treatment.

Open Source

V.E.I.N is an open-source integration and is accessible to all parts of the economic society.
